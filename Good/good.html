The Good

Aesthetic Apparatus

Aesthetic Apparatus's home page features a bold logo and header with four simple tabs beneath, making navigation of their portfolio and online poster store straightforward. The large graphic image cycles gracefully through a slideshow of their work images to intrigue visitors. Beneath the central image, company news and blog posts are featured. Overall, a clean but bold home page that's easy on the eyes.

Editions du LIC

Editions du LIC is a European photography art book publishing company. Their clean, bare bones layout relies on a series of drop-down tabs to lead visitors to find their favorite photographers and more information about the company. A simple search function, cart review for book purchases, and login is perched in the right corner.

Pitchfork

Pitchfork Media, whether you love or hate their brand politics and music ranking system, has an undeniably excellent website suited to their media needs. New album reviews are featured centrally, with recent music news on the left panel. Below the title banner, navigational links are boldly presented to lead visitors to news, reviews, best new music, and more. Overall, they have a really bold but clean news-format layout.
